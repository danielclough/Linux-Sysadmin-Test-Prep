### luks

`fdisk /dev/sdX`
`partprobe /dev/sdX`
`cryptsetup luksFormat /dev/sdX1`
`cryptsetup luksOpen /dev/sdX1 secret-disk`
`echo 'secret-disk   /dev/sdX1' | tee -a /etc/crypttab`
`mkfs.ext4 /dev/mapper/secret-disk`
`mkdir -p /secret`
`echo '/dev/mapper/secret-disk   /secret   ext4   defaults 1 2' | tee -a /etc/fstab`
`mount /secret`
