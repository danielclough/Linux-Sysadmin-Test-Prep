### logical volumes

1. `fdisk /dev/sdX` (t 8e; for each)
2. `partprobe -s` 
3. `pvcreate /dev/sdX  /dev/sdY`
    - `pvdisplay` or `pvs -v`
    - `wipefs /dev/sdX` may clear errors
4. `vgcreate <VG> /dev/sdX /dev/sdY` 
    - `vgdisplay -v` or `vgs`
5. `lvcreate -L <SIZE> -n <LV> <VG>` (-L fixed size; -l percentage of remaining space)
    - `lvdisplay` or `lvs`
6. `mkfs.ext4 /dev/<VG>/<LV>`
7. `mount /dev/<VG>/<LV> <MountPoint>`

- `lvresize -r -L <SIZE> <LV>`  (-L -2.5G; -l +100%FREE)
- `vgextend <VG> /dev/sdZ`

- `lvremove`, `vgremove`, `pvremove`
